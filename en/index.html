Jointly organized by Zhejiang University and China Tourist Attraction Association in collaboration with the United Nations World Tourism Organization (UNWTO), Pacific Asia Travel Association (PATA), and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, the International Smart Tourism Conference 2015 aims to establish a high-end communication platform for information sharing and exchange of views on the recent developmentsin smart tourism for relevant government sectors, research institutions, industry associations, tourism enterprises and financial institutions.

The International Smart TourismConference will be held on October 17-18, 2015 at the Crowne Plaza Hangzhou Xandadu Resort, Hangzhou China. The theme of the conference is “New Normal, New Travel, New Experience and New Paradigm”.Such topics as information technology and its applicationsin tourism, social media and tourism, tourism product innovations and tourism internet investment will be discussed at the conference. Leading industry executives, academics and government officials in the areas of e-Tourism,Internet development, financial investment will be invited to attend and share their views on smart tourism at the conference.
